AI is no longer a differentiator by itself. What matters is whether it solves a meaningful problem inside the program.

## Where it makes sense

Inside the Zegendia ecosystem, AI is useful when it helps:

- classify and clean reward catalogs
- detect duplicate or inconsistent items
- suggest rewards by audience or country
- identify fatigue or churn signals
- prioritize campaigns with stronger response potential

## Where it should not be exaggerated

AI does not replace weak operations. It will not magically fix an irrelevant catalog or poorly designed delivery workflows.

## The practical advantage

Used well, AI reduces operational friction and improves decision quality. That is why it makes sense inside Oh My Rewards and in the future of PuntosPlus: less manual noise and more clarity to execute.
