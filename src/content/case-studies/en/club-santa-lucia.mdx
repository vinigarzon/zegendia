## The challenge

Club Santa Lucía starts with a very practical question: how do you motivate hundreds of bakeries to buy more often, stay loyal to a brand, and engage with commercial campaigns without relying only on discounts?

The answer was not a generic app. It was a B2B system capable of recognizing purchases, assigning points, activating promotions, managing redemptions, and running clean operations across cities such as Quito, Guayaquil, and Cuenca.

## The solution

Zegendia designed a bakery-focused loyalty platform where every flour or supply purchase creates visible, redeemable value.

- Bakeries earn points based on purchases.
- The equivalency is configurable. A simple version is `1 point = 1 USD spent`.
- Points can be redeemed for physical rewards.
- The program runs through a participant portal and an administrative dashboard.

## What it includes

- Participant portal for balances, history, and reward browsing.
- Administrative dashboard for purchases, redemptions, and campaign visibility.
- POS or source-system integration.
- Promotions engine and configurable earning rules.
- Lightweight gamification to boost repeat behavior.
- Logistics and billing workflows.
- Fraud alerts and operational controls.
- Reporting and support.

## Why it matters

Club Santa Lucía is more than an attractive bakery case. It is an important commercial proof point for the new Zegendia ecosystem.

It proves that a loyalty model can be configured for a very specific vertical, with clear rules, physical rewards, and regional operations, without rebuilding everything from scratch each time.
